About Me

Hi! My given name is Danielle Sison, NUX is my pending tradename. I am a bootcamp student (Full-stack developer) at the University of Texas at Austin. This online portfolio showcases the projects I have worked on during my enrollment in the program and it serves as the basis of my capabilities within the scope of coding.

Within current professional boundaries, I am a drug dealer... legally. I bet the preceding statement shocked you initially, but I do have an explanation. My previous and current career background is in the world of pharmacy, to put it in plain text... I am a pharmacist. I've earned my credentials from Southeast Asia, and to be more precise, from the Philippines. I had graduated at the top of my class with the distinction of Cum Laude. In present time, I am working as a procurement and inventory coordinator for the company I work at for almost a decade.

I am pretty sure you are asking why am I going through this program? "You already have a beautiful career", as many say so. I can answer this question in many ways but if an honest answer is what you are seeking for, my response would be... it is because I want to change my career into something I truly love, into something that I am genuinely interested in, and into, what I believe, is my calling. Technology or computers has always been something that was easy for me to understand as a little lady. To put it simply, anything in this field naturally flows for me. Consequently, I want to be able to contribute to society by growth and development through this path.

Despite my realization into my true calling, I recognize that I have much to learn, and I have much to catch up on, in terms of learning how to code, or just to be merely up to date within this field. Nonetheless, I am open to expanding my intellect towards this area of expertise, and I continuously stay motivated for my family, my friends, and myself. Please continue on below to see the projects that I have worked on, thank you!
